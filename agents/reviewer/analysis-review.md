# Data Analysis Review Prompt

When reviewing a data analysis report for PetCare:
1. **Metric Accuracy:** Are the interpretations of data from @COMPANY.md or provided CSVs correct?
2. **Evidence Quality:** Does the report use direct quotes from @interviews?
3. **Actionability:** Are the recommendations specific enough for the engineering or product teams to act upon?
4. **Persona Alignment:** Do the insights correlate with the known pain points of our personas (Alex, Chloe, Jordan) in @PERSONAS.md?
5. **Impact:** Will following these recommendations improve our North Star Metric (Weekly Active Households with Updated Stock or Confirmed Refill)?

Provide a critique of the methodology and the conclusions.
