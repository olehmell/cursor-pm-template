When writing PRDs in this folder:
- Always check `@PRODUCT.md` to ensure the new feature aligns with our "Low-effort setup" and "At a glance clarity" principles.
- Reference `@COMPANY.md` when defining success metrics (activation, retention, North Star).
- Ask the questions in [socratic-questioning](./socratic-questioning.md) before writing the PRD, and do not continue until the user answers them.
- Add to plan do stakeholders review `./reviewers` in separate artifact

Prefered frameworks:
- for priotisation use `./templates/KYIV-framework.md`
- for PRD writing use `./templates/Lennys-PRD-Template.md`
