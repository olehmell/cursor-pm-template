# PetCare Company Overview


PetCare is building a mobile-first pet companion app that helps pet owners **plan feeding**, **avoid “out of food” moments**, and (later) **order pet food on time** through local partners.

## Company Mission

**Mission:** Help pet owners keep their pets healthy and cared for by making daily routines effortless.  
**Vision:** Become the default “pet life” app in Eastern Europe, starting with feeding and recurring care.

**Values:**
- **Pet-first:** Every decision starts with pet wellbeing (and owner peace of mind)
- **Less guilt, more control:** Reduce “I forgot” moments with gentle, useful nudges
- **Simple UX:** Fast setup, low effort, clear status at a glance
- **Local partnerships:** Win by integrating into local ecosystems (shops, later clinics)
- **Move fast:** Validate quickly, iterate weekly, cut what doesn’t work

## Market Position

### Target Market

**Primary:** Urban pet owners (Kyiv first), especially:
- First-time pet owners (information-heavy, high anxiety)

- Households with multiple caretakers (shared responsibility)

**Secondary (later):**
- Pet shops (partner channel)

## Product Strategy (Why We’re Starting With Food)

We intentionally start with “food planning” because it’s:
- **Recurring** (weekly/monthly)
- **Emotionally loaded** (owners feel guilt when they forget)
- **Actionable** (reminders → reorder → delivery)

Long-term we expand into higher-LTV areas (health records, vet workflows, consumables beyond food), but we need a wedge with strong retention first.

## Key Terminology

**Pet Profile:** A pet (species/breed/age/weight) with routines and history  
**Feeding Plan:** Food type(s) + daily grams + servings schedule  
**Stock:** “How much food is left” model used to estimate days remaining  
**Caretakers:** Multiple people in one household sharing the same pet profile  
**Reorder CTA:** Any in-app action to start “get more food” (click-out or partner checkout)
